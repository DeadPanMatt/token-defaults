# Reporting an Issue

Found something broken, or got an idea for an improvement? Great - open
an issue at
[github.com/DeadPanMatt/token-presets/issues][issues] and use the
template below as a starting point. The more detail you can give, the
quicker it'll get sorted.

You don't have to fill in every section - just the ones that make sense
for what you're reporting. Skip the rest.

[issues]: https://github.com/DeadPanMatt/token-presets/issues

---

## Template - copy this into your new issue

*A one-line summary. E.g. "Disposition isn't applying when I use the
toolbar button on selected tokens."*

### What were you trying to do?

*Describe what you were doing in plain English. Walk through the steps
if it helps. E.g. "I made a preset called Hostile Monster with
disposition set to Hostile. I selected three goblin tokens on the
canvas, clicked the user-gear toolbar icon, picked Hostile Monster from
the dialog and clicked Apply."*

### What did you expect to happen?

*E.g. "The three goblins should now have a Hostile disposition."*

### What actually happened?

*E.g. "Nothing visibly changed. The disposition is still Neutral when I
